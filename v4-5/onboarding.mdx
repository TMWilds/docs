### Creating Account

Go to the <a href="https://portal.ditto.live/" target="_blank">Ditto portal</a> and sign up:

1. Enter your information and password.

2. If you want, select to **Enroll in multi‑factor authentication**.&#x20;

3. Read and select to agree to the <a href="https://www.ditto.live/legal/terms-of-service" target="_blank">**Terms of Service**</a>.&#x20;
   &#x20;
4. Complete signup:

   - To sign up using Google Single Sign-On, select **Sign up with Google**.

   - To sign up using the email entered in the previous step, click **Create Account**.

### Creating Your App

Once you've created your Ditto account and logged in to the portal, try on Ditto with up to two apps that you create in the portal.&#x20;

To create an app:&#x20;

1. Click **Create Organization**.

2. Enter the **Name **of your organization and, if available, the **Organization Website URL**.

3. Click **Create Organization**.

4. Click **Let's Create an App!**.

5. Enter a **Name **and a slug for your **App URL**.

6. Select your **App Visibility **Preference.

7. Click **Create App**.
