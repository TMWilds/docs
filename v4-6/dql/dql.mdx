DQL is designed to simplify data interactions in your applications, providing a consistent and intuitive interface for querying and manipulating data. Whether you're a developer looking to streamline your backend operations or an engineer focused on optimizing data flow, DQL is tailored to meet your needs.

## DQL Quick Reference

### [SELECT](/ab-redirect-url?docId=iMDPwvQgVj4yyV5RalGbi&isHosted=true&queryTemplate=&shareableToken=&hostName=docs.ditto.live&docAnchorId=)

```sql DQL
SELECT *
FROM your_collection_name
[WHERE condition]
[ORDER BY orderby_expression_1, orderby_expression_2, ... [ASC|DESC]]
[LIMIT limit_value]
[OFFSET number_of_documents_to_skip]
```

### [INSERT](/ab-redirect-url?docId=EMhcouL5Kbus5z05x_pKu&isHosted=true&queryTemplate=&shareableToken=&hostName=docs.ditto.live&docAnchorId=)

```sql DQL
INSERT INTO your_collection_name
DOCUMENTS ([document1]),([document2]), ([document3]), ...
[ON ID CONFLICT [FAIL | DO NOTHING | DO UPDATE]]
```

### [UPDATE](/ab-redirect-url?docId=_vxfd7_jJFfTzByIVqoxh&isHosted=true&queryTemplate=&shareableToken=&hostName=docs.ditto.live&docAnchorId=)

```sql DQL
UPDATE your_collection_name
SET field1 = value1, field2 -> [mutator], ...
WHERE [condition]
```

### [EVICT](/ab-redirect-url?docId=ksPbI9CsHPdud0iyuow9E&isHosted=true&queryTemplate=&shareableToken=&hostName=docs.ditto.live&docAnchorId=)

```sql DQL
EVICT FROM your_collection_name
WHERE [condition]
```
