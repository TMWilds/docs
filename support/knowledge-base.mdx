## Welcome to the Ditto Knowledge Base

At Ditto, we’re committed to helping you get the most out of our products and services. Whether you're just getting started or you're a seasoned pro, our Knowledge Base is here to provide you with all the resources and solutions you need.

### What You’ll Find Here:

Our Knowledge Base is divided into several categories, each designed to help you quickly find answers to your questions or solutions to any issues you might encounter. Here's a quick overview of what you can explore:

#### 1. **Troubleshooting Guides**

Having trouble? Our troubleshooting guides walk you through resolving common issues with clear, detailed steps. From connectivity problems to software errors, we’ve got you covered.

#### 2. **Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)**

Find answers to some of the most frequently asked questions. This section covers a wide range of topics, from basic product functionality to advanced use cases.

#### **3.  Technical Best Practices**

Encountered an error code? Discover valuable tips, tricks, and best practices to elevate your Ditto experience and conquer common errors. Our expert advice is tailored to boost your efficiency and safeguard data integrity, empowering you to achieve success!

#### 4. MongoDB Partnership

Interested in learning more about migrating from MongoDB? We are still in the development phase for most features but have created getting started guides for those seeking the basics.

#### 5. Glossary

Read an unfamiliar word? Check out our Glossary! A list of key terms and definitions related to our product and service. It helps users understand technical jargon, acronyms, or specific terms you may encounter while troubleshooting or reading our support articles.

### How to Use the Knowledge Base:

1. **Search by Keywords:** Use the search bar to quickly find articles related to your query. Just type in a keyword or a phrase, and relevant articles will appear.
2. **Explore Categories:** Browse through our categories if you’re looking for information on a specific topic or product.
3. **Follow Step-by-Step Guides:** Our articles are structured to help you solve issues step-by-step, with easy-to-follow instructions and screenshots where applicable.
4. **Give Feedback:** Found the answer helpful? Let us know! At the end of each article, you’ll have the opportunity to provide feedback so we can continue improving our resources.

### Need More Help?

If you can’t find the answer you’re looking for, don’t worry! You can always reach out to our support team. You can <a href="https://docs.ditto.live/support" target="_blank">submit a support ticket</a>, and we’ll be happy to assist you.
