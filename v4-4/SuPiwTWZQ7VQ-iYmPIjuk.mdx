Welcome to the _Platform Manual_, the ultimate source to understanding Ditto concepts, implementing best practices, and following step-by-step instructions.

Use this manual to learn the intricacies of Ditto, become proficient in read-and-write operations, master advanced querying techniques, and more.

### About[](#KU9ae)

This manual is developer-focused and designed to accommodate a range of coding skill levels.

So whether you're new to decentralized network infrastructure and distributed systems like Ditto or merely seeking to enhance your existing skills and knowledge, this manual is intended for you.

### Assumptions[](#29ANz)

This _Platform Manual_ assumes that you have read and fully understand the information provided in [Ditto Basics](/v4-4/ditto-basics).
